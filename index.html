üìã Introduction

The DRR VIS application is a mobile weighing system operated by the Department of Rural Roads, Ministry of Transport, used for recording vehicle weighing data and inspecting overweight vehicles.

This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when using the DRR VIS application.

üì¶ Information We Collect

2.1 Location Data

  • GPS Coordinates: To record the location of weighing stations (SPOT) and vehicle inspection sites
  • Current Location Display: To show user's position on the in-app map

2.2 Vehicle Inspection Data

  • Photos: Images of vehicles being inspected and weighed
  • Weight Data: Total weight, axle weight, and weighing details
  • License Plate: Vehicle registration number and province
  • Date & Time: Inspection timestamp

2.3 User Account Information

  • Username: For system login
  • Officer Information: Name, department, position
  • Profile Picture: Officer's photo (if provided)

üéØ Purpose of Data Use

‚ö†Ô∏è Important: Location data is collected only while you are using the app

We do not collect location data when the app is closed or running in the background

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • Record and store vehicle inspection and weighing data
  • Generate inspection reports and operational statistics
  • Verify inspection location and time
  • Monitor and prevent vehicle overloading
  • Improve service quality and operational efficiency
  • Analyze data for planning and rural road system development

üîí Data Security

We prioritize the security of your data and implement the following security measures:

  • Data Encryption: All data is encrypted during transmission using HTTPS/SSL protocol
  • Access Control: Only authorized personnel can access the data
  • Security Systems: Servers equipped with modern Firewall and Anti-virus systems
  • Data Backup: Automated regular data backup system
  • Security Audits: Regular security checks and system updates

ü§ù Data Sharing

We do not share your personal information with third parties, except in the following cases:

  • Your Consent: When we have your explicit consent
  • Legal Requirement: When required by law or court order
  • Government Agencies: Sharing with relevant government agencies for official duties
  • Safety Protection: To prevent or mitigate danger to life, body, or health

Note: We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties for commercial purposes

‚öñÔ∏è Your Rights

You have rights under Thailand's Personal Data Protection Act B.E. 2562 (2019):

  • Right to Access: Access and request copies of your personal data
  • Right to Rectification: Correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of personal data (subject to legal conditions)
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent for data processing
  • Right to Restriction: Request temporary suspension of data use
  • Right to Data Portability: Receive data in machine-readable format
  • Right to Object: Object to processing of your personal data
  • Right to Complain: File complaints with the Personal Data Protection Committee

‚è±Ô∏è Data Retention Period

We retain your personal data for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes stated in this policy or as required by law.

  • Vehicle Inspection Data: Retained according to government document retention regulations
  • User Account Data: Retained during active use and 2 years after account deletion
  • Log Data: Retained for 90 days for security purposes
